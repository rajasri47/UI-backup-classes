Todays News Content

News Item 1:

Heavy rain inundated parts of Hyderabad last evening, causing massive traffic jams. Accompanied by lightning and thunderstorms, the sudden spell of rain resulted in waterlogging in the low-lying areas of the city while Secunderabad was largely spared.Gachibowli, Kondapur, Jubilee Hills, Nampally, and Dilsukhnagar were among the areas that received heavy rains. Roads in Tolichowki were flooded. Neighbouring Yadadri Bhuvanagiri too received heavy downpours.
